About me

I'm a full-stack engineer with a strong background in building and scaling SaaS applications. Over the years, I've worked with Ruby on Rails, JavaScript (React, ES6, CoffeeScript, jQuery), and various backend technologies, delivering high-impact features for event management, collaboration, and customer engagement platforms.

Experience

Most recently, I was a Full Stack Engineer at Concept3D, where I played a key role in developing the Localist event management platform. I worked on customer-facing templates using liquid, administrative templates using ERB and React, and API integrations with such platforms as LinkedIn and X, while improving platform usability and efficiency. Before that, I was part of Localist, where I co-developed email campaign automations, social media promotion tools, and RESTful APIs to enhance event data accessibility. I've also led projects at No.Inc, Teaching Strategies, and ASPPH, where I helped improve application performance, optimized deployment processes, and introduced automated testing to reduce regressions. I've partnered closely with other teams throughout my career, ensuring solutions aligned with goals while mentoring junior developers.
